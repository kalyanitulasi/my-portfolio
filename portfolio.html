PORTFOLIO

Family Photography

I am passionate about capturing the beautiful moments and emotions shared between family members. With candid and emotional portraits, I strive to tell your family's unique story in a heartfelt way.

During our family photography session, I will guide you to the best lighting and help you feel relaxed and comfortable in front of the camera. There are no awkward poses or forced smiles, just natural and genuine moments that reflect your family's love and joy.

Maternity Photography

Maternity photography is the perfect way to commemorate your beautiful baby bump and mark the beginning of this exciting and life-changing journey. Our sessions are designed to be both relaxing and fun, and we welcome partners and children to join in the photoshoot. We offer a range of dresses for your session, but you're also welcome to wear your own favorites. Most clients schedule their maternity photography sessions 3 to 4 weeks before their due date, but some prefer to come in at around 25 weeks instead.

Corporate Portraits

Your Professional Headshot is your digital introduction to potential employers, clients, and partners, leaving a strong impression. I craft Headshots that exude confidence and professionalism, whether for personal or corporate use.

The session is to capture your unique personality and personal brand. Whether you're in business or entrepreneurship, I ensure you look your best in a relaxed environment. Let me elevate your personal brand and career with a compelling Headshot that makes a lasting impact, both in your professional and corporate endeavors.

Baby Photography

Capture cherished memories of your little one. You have the flexibility to select the age milestone that suits you and your baby best; there's no fixed month. Many of my clients opt for sessions around the 3-month mark, often referred to as the '100 days' celebration or 1st Birthday.

My expertise is preserving your baby's individuality. I'm dedicated to conveying the pure joy and love that parents and siblings share through kisses and affection to the baby.

Throughout the session, your baby will be dressed in delightful outfits and adorned with adorable accessories.

Model Photography

Take a look at my Model Photography showcase featuring carefully curated style photoshoots with models. I invest a lot of thought into each session, planning creative concepts and selecting props and wardrobe to highlight each model's unique personality.
